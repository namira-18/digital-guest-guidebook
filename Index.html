Arrival Information

Check-in Instructions

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM. Self check-in is available with the key lockbox. The lockbox code will be sent to you on the day of arrival.

Getting Here

From Cape Town International Airport:

  • By car: 45-minute drive via M3 and M5 highways
  • By taxi/Uber: Approximately 500-600 ZAR

Parking Information

Free on-site parking is available for one vehicle. Please park in the designated area marked with your unit number.
